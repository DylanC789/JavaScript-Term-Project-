------------------------------
    GAME DESCRIPTIONS
------------------------------
1)   Christmas Tree Decorating Arrange Ornaments and Presents in order to decorate an Evergreen Tree, While Santa can be seen flying off in the distance.
2)  Christmas Tic Tac Toe with Candy Canes and Weaths. Be the first to connect 3 to win. 
3)  A Snowballs Chance, play as a snowball doging pillers of flame. See how long you can last.
